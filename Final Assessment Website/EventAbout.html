OUR STORY

Simply Eventful came to be when the Co-CEOs encountered a single mom who was
eagerly preparing for her daughter’s birthday party. She envisioned a magical day
filled with laughter and fun. However, amidst her busy days of errands and work
deadlines, the tasks and logistics of planning decorations, games, and activities felt
overwhelming and daunting, particularly with the need to stay within her budget.
With the help of our Co-CEOs and the successful creation of the event, Simply
Eventful was brought to pass to bring that hope and joy to more individuals.
